Chocolate Cake

This classic, one-bowl chocolate cake is celebrated for being incredibly moist, dark, and easy to whip up without any fancy equipment.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C. Grease and flour 23cm round cake pans.
  2. Whisk the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in large bowl.
  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
